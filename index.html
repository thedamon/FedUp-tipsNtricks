Copy any expression result in the Chrome or Firefox dev tools: 

copy(['a,b,c'].split(','))
to get the actual array you can paste anywhere you like. Or if you've previously run an expression, and want the result without re-running it, use 
copy($_)

General 😸

Provide a comment anytime you use a regexp or what appears to be an arbitrary value

Treat your code and process like a UX project where the users are other developers

Don’t be hard on yourself, but be just a tiny bit hard on yourself.

As developers we live in the hard things, it can be demoralizing or frustrating. That’s good, it means you want to get better.

Take a break if you’re going in circles. Let it coagulate. Take a step back.

Rubberducking: explain your problem to a coworker, your mom, your coffeecup, or a rubber duck.

Quit while you’re in the middle of something to start the day hungry

Embrace failure and ignorance, don’t be a afraid to say you don’t know or ask for help.
